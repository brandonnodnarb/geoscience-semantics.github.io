Lightweight Semantic Models

Dictionaries

Basic sources of geoscience terminology. There are many versions of resources that could populate this section. These are provided for example only to illustrate overall comprehensiveness.

  • Dictionary of Geologic Terms - A hard copy dictionary.
  • Dictionary of Geology & Earth Sciences - A hard copy dictionary.
  • General Dictionary of Geology - A general dictionary of geology.

Glossaries

  • Glossary of Geology - Hard copy glossary. (Also available as a paid app on iTunes and Google Play)
  • GSL Glossary of Terms - The Geological Society's glossary of terms.

Term lists

Lists of terms or vocabularies; primarily used for tagging. AGU Index of Terms - Closed set of terms used by the American Geophysical Unioon (AGU) to categorize conference and publication keywords. Global Change Master Directory - Terms for tagging NASA's Earth Science data and services.
